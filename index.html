The way I work

Five principles I do not compromise on.

01

Understand before recommending.

I do not prescribe a website, SEO or ads before I understand what is actually getting in the way.

02

Fix causes, not symptoms.

More traffic will not repair a confusing offer. A redesign will not repair a broken follow-up system.

03

Never sell what is not needed.

Selling air may close a deal. It does not build the reputation I want.

04

Keep difficult things simple.

Clients often thank me for answering every question in a way that is easy to understand and act on.

05

Never promise sales.

I promise honest thinking, clear communication and the strongest practical path I can find.
